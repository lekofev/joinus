JOIN US has been created to unite cultures all over the world. Through this page, out of country visitors
and Peruvians will be able to share ideas, precious moments, opinions, experiences, language and more. If you are interested in making new friends and broaden your network, join us by doing what you like the most.

Lima is known as “The City of Kings”, in this city you can treasure a diversity of historical centers still existing from the age of Peruvian Viceroyalty. Visit our most important city touristic places with fellows who know the streets of Lima and who are willing to guide you through must-see places.

Share conversations about Lima City’s history or just join our group and enjoy the landscape with Peruvian and out of country visitors eager to know about Lima. Join this group and know more, enjoy more and learn more about ancient and modern Lima.
“...with fellows who know the streets of Lima and who are willing to guide you through must-see places”
